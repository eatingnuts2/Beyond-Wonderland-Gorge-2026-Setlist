A sleek, mobile-first schedule planner designed to help you navigate **FVDED In The Park 2026** without missing the sets that matter most.

Built as a lightweight web app that works **offline**, so even when signal disappears at the festival… your plan doesn’t.

---

## 🎧 What this is

This is an interactive timeline of all FVDED In The Park sets across **2 days** and **4 stages**:

* Pacific Stage
* Northwest Tent
* Forest Stage
* Propagate Stage

It turns the full festival schedule into a tool for actually **deciding where to go**.

Instead of relying on static schedule images, you can:

* Visually map each day
* Build your own schedule
* Quickly adjust on the fly
* Keep track of conflicts between priority sets
* Search artists instantly

---

## ✨ Features

### 🎯 Smart set selection

Tap sets to cycle through:

* ☆ **Interested** — backup option
* ★ **Must-see** — your priority
* Off — remove

This makes it easy to plan while staying flexible.

---

### ⚠️ Intelligent clash detection

Clashes only appear when it actually matters:

* ★ Must-see vs ★ Must-see → **CLASH warning**
* ☆ Interested sets → **never marked as clashes**

👉 This keeps your backup options visible without clutter.

---

### 🧭 My Plan mode

Tap **My Plan** to:

* Fade out unselected sets
* Keep your plan directly on the timeline
* See your festival schedule exactly as it plays out

---

### 🔍 Artist search

Quickly find any artist and jump straight to their set.

---

### 🕒 Timeline view

The planner keeps the full FVDED schedule in a visual timeline format, making it easier to see:

* Who overlaps
* Which stages are close in time
* Where your must-see conflicts are
* What backup sets fit between priorities

---

### 💾 Offline support

Once opened, the app works **without internet**:

* Perfect for festival grounds where signal can be unreliable
* Add to Home Screen for a smoother app-like experience
* Your saved selections stay on your device

---

## 🚀 How to use

1. Open the site
2. Add it to your Home Screen
3. Tap sets to build your plan
4. Use **My Plan** to focus your schedule
5. Watch for Must-see clashes
6. Stay flexible between stages

---

## 📡 Offline usage

Before heading to the festival:

* Open the app once while online
* Make your plan
* Then it will continue to work offline

For extra safety:

* Screenshot your plan
* Or keep a backup copy of the HTML
